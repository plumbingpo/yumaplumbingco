Restaurant Plumbing

Restaurant and Food Service Plumbing

Food service businesses place extreme demands on their plumbing systems, far beyond what residential systems face. High-volume kitchens generate enormous amounts of grease, food waste, and wastewater that can overwhelm standard plumbing systems. Yuma Plumbing Co specializes in restaurant plumbing services including grease trap installation, sizing, and maintenance; commercial garbage disposal systems; three-compartment sink installation to meet health code requirements; floor drain maintenance and cleaning; and hot water system design to meet the high-volume demands of a commercial kitchen.

Grease trap compliance is one of the most critical plumbing issues for food service operators in Yuma. Local regulations require grease traps or interceptors to prevent fats, oils, and grease from entering the municipal sewer system. An undersized, poorly maintained, or malfunctioning grease trap can result in code violations, fines, and sewer backups that force you to close your doors. We install properly sized grease traps for new restaurants and provide scheduled maintenance services to keep existing systems compliant and functional. Our maintenance program includes pumping, cleaning, and inspection on a schedule that meets regulatory requirements and prevents problems before they occur.

Commercial dishwashers, ice machines, and beverage systems all require proper plumbing connections with backflow prevention. We install and maintain these connections to ensure they meet health codes and operate reliably. When equipment breaks down, our fast response time helps minimize disruption to your business operations.

Backflow Prevention

Backflow Prevention Testing and Certification

Backflow prevention devices are required on most commercial properties in Yuma to protect the public water supply from contamination. These devices prevent water from flowing backward through the plumbing system, which could introduce pollutants, chemicals, or bacteria into the municipal water supply. Local water authorities require annual testing and certification of backflow prevention devices by a certified tester.

Yuma Plumbing Co employs certified backflow prevention testers who can install new devices, perform required annual testing, make repairs to failed devices, and submit certification paperwork to the local water authority on your behalf. We maintain testing records and send reminders when your annual test is due, ensuring you stay in compliance and avoid penalties. Failing to maintain required backflow prevention can result in fines and potential water service disconnection.
