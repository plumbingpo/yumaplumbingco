Signs You Need Emergency Plumbing in Yuma

Recognizing the warning signs early can save you time, money, and prevent significant damage to your property. Watch for these telltale signs that professional help is needed:

  • Water gushing from a burst or broken pipe anywhere in your home
  • Sewage backing up through drains, toilets, or floor drains
  • Complete loss of water pressure or no water at all
  • Gas smell near water heater, gas lines, or plumbing fixtures
  • Flooding from a failed sump pump during heavy rain
  • Water heater leaking large amounts of water or making unusual noises
  • Frozen pipes that have burst or are at risk of bursting

How to Prevent Emergency Plumbing Problems

A little prevention goes a long way in avoiding expensive plumbing repairs. Here are our expert recommendations:

  • Know where your main water shut-off valve is located and test it annually to ensure it works properly
  • Insulate exposed pipes in unheated areas like garages, crawl spaces, and attics to prevent freezing
  • Never pour grease, oil, or food waste down drains â€” these are the leading cause of drain clogs and backups
  • Schedule annual plumbing inspections to catch small problems before they become emergencies
  • Install water leak detectors near water heaters, washing machines, and under sinks for early warning
  • Keep a basic plumbing emergency kit with a pipe wrench, plumber's tape, and bucket accessible
  • Have your sewer line inspected with a camera every 3-5 years to prevent unexpected backups
